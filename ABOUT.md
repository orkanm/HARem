# The Heart of HARem

The name **HARem** is more than just a technical acronym (**H**ome **A**ssistant **Rem**ote); it is an homage to the historical and cultural "inner sanctum" of the household.

## üìõ Naming Inspiration: The Inner Sanctum
In Turkish and Ottoman history, the **Harem** represented the most private, sacred, and protected part of the home. It was the heart of domestic life, managed with absolute privacy and care. 

By adopting this name, the project acknowledges the smart home as the modern individual's "inner sanctum." Just as the historical Harem was the center of the household, this remote serves as the private, primary interface to control and protect the sanctity of your living space.

## üõ†Ô∏è Hardware Specifications
While the software is a lightweight "Thin Client," the hardware is chosen for a premium, tactile experience:
- **MCU**: ESP32-C3 Super Mini (RISC-V)
- **Display**: SSD1306/SH1106 128x64 OLED
- **Input**: EC11 Rotary Encoder with Push-button
- **Battery Management**: 
    - LiPo/Li-ion support via GPIO0 voltage sensing.
    - Hardware-triggered wakeup on GPIO4.
    - Software-controlled deep sleep for multi-week standby.
